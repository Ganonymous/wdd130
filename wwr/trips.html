Popular Trips

Name Duration Group Size Cost Description
Deep Woods Delvers 2 days 12-18 $700 + $75/each Not a particularly challenging route, but lots of beautiful scenery. One dinner and breakfast each included
Hike & Float 4 hours 6-10 $300 + $40/each Carry a raft to the launch point for one of the most exciting routes. Great for team building.
Training Turbulence 2 hours 4-8 $200 + $40/each Our beginner course. Includes a lesson for the first 30 minutes.
Three Teams Race & Raft 4 hours 18+ $450 + $50/each Divide into teams for a long course, alternating between chaotic rapids and wide calm stretches to race. Schedule at least a month in advance for parties of 30 or more.
Whirling Waves 3 hours 6-8 $325 + $40/each A chaotic advanced course, where currents are likely to turn a boat backwards at least once. Not for the faint of heart!
Paddle & Plunge 1 hour 4-6 $175 + $40/each A shorter route, containing several plunges, making this route comparable to a log flume!
Self-Guided Expert Course 2.5 hours 4-8 $150 + $45/each Skilled Rafters Only! Rent a raft and gear, and take on the rapids without a guide, relying on your own experience to choose your path. Try not to get too soaked!
Tangled Trials 3.5 hours 4-8 $400 + $50/each These rapids form a rapidly-changing whitewater delta. Pay close attention to your guide, every trip is wildly different!
The Grand Tour 3 days 6-8 $1500 + $125/each Challenge all our routes in one fell swoop! Two dinners and breakfasts each will be provided. Only for the confident and brave!
