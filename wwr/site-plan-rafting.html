Overview

Purpose

The purpose is two-fold. First, bring in additional customers through web traffic, both in the context of out-of-towners looking to go rafting, and locals who hadn't been before. Second, streamline the process of getting someone from interested to on the water, by making information readily available, and allowing for bookings to be made from anywhere.

Audience

The target audience is primarily youth groups and young adults, either in the local area, or close enough to make an excursion of it, i.e. young mens High Adventure. Q1: What do they know about white-water rafting? A: Some customers will already be familiar with the activity, but there will also be a significant portion who have barely heard of it. Q2: What do they want to do on this site? A: Determine if they want to go rafting with this company, and if so, book their trip. Q3: How will the site be accessed? A: Usually on a home computer while planning a trip, but sometimes from a mobile device when re-arranging plans that went awry.
