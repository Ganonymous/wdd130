Washington

Washington is the northwesternmost state in the lower 48. It is divided by the Cascade Mountains into the dryer east and the temperate rainforest west. Honestly, all I know about eastern Washington is that you have to get past it to go to Idaho. Much of the southern border (with Oregon) is the Columbia River, which created a gorge with lots of cool hiking opportunities and waterfalls where it cuts through the Cascades.
